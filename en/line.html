Discover the legendary James Bond line that embodies the elegance, mystery and charm of cinema's most famous secret agent.

A memorable introduction

In the world of cinema, certain lines become emblematic, transcending the status of mere words to become cinematic icons in their own right. Like the famous "Je suis ton père" ("I am your father"), "My name is Bond, Jame Bond" has gained a cult following over the years. A line that embodies mystery, danger and class.

The origin of the legend

This line was first uttered in 1962 in the franchise's first film: James Bond vs. Dr. No". Actor Sean Connery delivered the line perfectly, heralding the arrival of a mysterious spy on screen, ready to do anything for the British crown. This line has the power to evoke a mystery, a timeless and poignant charm, thanks to the actors' performances throughout the saga. Each one pronounces it in their own way, adding their own interpretation to the emblematic character that is James Bond

First use of the line by Sean Connery

The Evolution of James Bond

Over the years, various actors have played James Bond. The line "My Name is Bond, James Bond" has become recurrent in every film. From Sean Connery to Daniel Craig, via Roger Moore, Pierce Brosnan and others, the line has evolved while retaining its immutable charm, and continues to make an impact in cinemas.

A Symbol of Popular Culture

As mentioned above, the line has acquired a cult following over the years, and has become much more than an introduction to the character. It represents the entire universe of the saga, from the elegance of the character to the charm of the films.
