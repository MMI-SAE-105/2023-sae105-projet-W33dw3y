About me

If you've arrived here, it's because you want to know a little more about this website.

This website is a project carried out as part of a teaching exercise at the MMI department in Montbéliard. My name is Mathis Liegeon, I'm in my first year at BUT and I'm the creator of this website. I wrote and designed all the pages of this site, inspired by the James Bond saga. On this site, I wanted to convey the atmosphere of this saga of which I'm a fan. I've tried to provide the best possible experience through the texts, colors and visuals on the site
